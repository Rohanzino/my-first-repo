Flooding situation across the Sunamganj, Netrokona, Mymensingh and Sherpur districts, especially the haor basin in the country, is feared to deteriorate due to incessant raining and overflows from upstream.Inundation at the floodplains left hundreds of dwellers marooned and in some parts of the haor region, road communications were snapped. Farmers fear that water logging might damage their crop fields. Water level at Sunamganj Sadar, Bishwambharpur and Tahirpur upazila is rising. Overflows from upstream inundated the Sunamganj-Tahirpur road, snapping the inter-district road communication. If the water level continues to rise, low lands across Sunamganj would be flooded entirely within next couple of days, said Bangladesh Water Development Board (BWDB) officials, adding that Sunamganj and Cherapunji of India at upstream witnessed incessant rain for last three days.
